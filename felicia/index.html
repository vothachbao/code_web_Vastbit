About Us

VASTbit is a Vietnam based software outsourcing and solution provider company.
We offer outsourced product development, custom development, IT outsourcing services and with highly focus on qualitative, timely delivery and cost-effective.

MOBILE DEVELOPMENT

We focus on iPhone, iPad and Android platforms and develop many mobile applications for clients, from standalone application to complex, client-server applications

OFFSHORE DEV. CENTER

We also provide offshore development center (ODC) for our client. It is a helpful model that can help our clients to archive high return on investment and highest quality of software delivery

SOFTWARE DEVELOPMENT

We have developed various desktop applications, web portals and web applications for clients in some industry domains including HR&Recruitment, E-Commerce, Healthcare, Education, Communications, Finance, and more.

Our vision is to become a leading technology company, a successful long term partner for our Clients and known as a trusted provider in the outsourcing industry.
